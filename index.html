Zero Custody

We never touch your money, keys, or messages.

01dMonk is fully non-custodial. Here's what that means.

💰

We never hold your money

Every bet goes directly into a Solana escrow vault — a smart contract, not our bank account. We can't move it, freeze it, or take a cut beyond the transparent platform fee. The code pays out the winners. Not us.

🔑

Your wallet, your keys

Coinbase creates your embedded wallet — non-custodial from day one. No seed phrases to manage, but the keys are yours, not ours. We never have access to sign transactions on your behalf.

🔒

Your messages, your eyes only

End-to-end encrypted chat means the server stores ciphertext only. We couldn't read your banter even if we wanted to. Group keys rotate when members leave. No forwarding. No server-side previews.

Security

The vault doesn't care who you trust.

It enforces the rules regardless.

Escrow

Every market creates a Solana vault PDA. Stakes are escrowed instantly. Payouts settle in under 2 seconds with Ed25519 authority signatures. Real-time, verifiable, irreversible.

Five-Role Keys

Server authority signs. Ops wallet pays gas. Guardian can pause. Admin runs the group. Treasury receives passively. No single key can steal.

Bitmap Claims

Each payout tracked by u32 bitmap per bettor index. Double-claims impossible at the programme level. The contract is the referee.

Verifiable

The Anchor programme is deployed on-chain. Audit the code, verify the deployment. Parimutuel maths — transparent, no hidden edge.
